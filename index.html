Welcome To Hunter's Journal

Hunter's Journal is an unofficial wiki made by fan. In this wiki provided every monsters, locale, equipment, and trivias around the world of Monster Hunter. This wiki will be updated continuously. Stay tune!

Monster Hunter Wiki, By Fans, For Fans.

Monster Hunter universe is a vast universe where there are many monster species that live within. As a hunter, we're really get excited with amount of monsters and locales that can be explored and equipments to try onto the battlefield. This wiki is a way to enjoy the game's universe by acknowledging world of Monster Hunter.

Monsters

There are so many monsters that you can hunt in Monster Hunter. All of them has their own uniqueness, weakness, ailments, etc. make sure to know your monster before going in the hunt!

Equipments

Hunt your monster and craft the equipments! weapons, armors, even accessories require monster parts as its materials. Break monster's specific part and (hopefully) get the material needed!

Locale

Locales all-around the Monster Hunter universe inhabited by monsters. some monsters are specific to certain locale, some are invading another locale in order to satisfy their hunger.
